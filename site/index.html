Our Approach

AI-Driven Discovery Platform

Our platform integrates human genetic evidence, structural biology, and machine learning to identify and validate drug targets — compressing a multi-year discovery process into months.

Genetic Evidence Scoring

We rank disease targets by the strength of human genetic validation — prioritising proteins where nature has already confirmed biological relevance.

Structure-Based Screening

Using high-resolution protein structures, we computationally screen millions of small molecule candidates to identify those with the strongest binding potential.

ADMET Prediction

Before any wet-lab investment, we filter candidates for predicted absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion, and toxicity — focusing resources on developable molecules.

Target Identification

We begin by mining large-scale human genetic and genomic datasets to identify proteins with the strongest causal links to disease. By combining evidence from population genetics, rare variant studies, and gene expression data, we build a high-confidence target list before committing to any chemistry.

From Computation to the Clinic

Our computational pipeline generates validated small molecule candidates ready for IND-enabling studies. We partner with specialist CROs for in vitro and in vivo validation, with a target timeline of IND-enabling studies in 2026–2027 for our lead program.
